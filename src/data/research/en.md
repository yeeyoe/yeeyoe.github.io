I study Kähler geometry, focusing on the canonical metrics on Kähler manifolds and algebraic stabilities, and related problems on toric varieties. I am also interested in the non-Archimedean geometry, especially its recent applications in Kähler geometry.

### Some keywords:

Yau--Tian--Donaldson conjecture, toric varieties, Berkovich analytifications.
